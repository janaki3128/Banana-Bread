Take 1 1/2 cup wheat flour
1 cup sugar
4 mashed bananas
Mix everything well
Then add vannila essence
Baking soda 
Baking powder
Milk , oil and pinch of salt
Mix everything well...If required add more milk
Transfer the ingredients into a greased bread baking tray
Pre-heat the oven at 150 degrees for 10 minutes
Bake the cake for 40 mins at the same temperature
